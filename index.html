About SMDC

SM Development Corporation (SMDC), known as “The Good Guys,” is one of the leading real estate developers in the Philippines, committed to building a nation of homeowners. As part of SM Residences and a wholly owned subsidiary of SM Prime, SMDC develops master-planned communities that offer modern, well-designed residences in prime locations near SM malls, transport hubs, and key business districts—ensuring seamless connectivity and urban convenience. With a growing portfolio of integrated developments, SMDC is dedicated to creating sustainable, high-value communities that enhance quality of life, promote economic growth, and support urban progress. Its commitment to sustainability is reflected in its focus on efficient land use, green spaces, and energy-efficient building practices—shaping communities and building the good life.
